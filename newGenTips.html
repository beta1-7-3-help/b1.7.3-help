Here are some things to note Jumping From Modern Minecraft To b1.7.3:
1. Don't use the vannila Launcher 2. Hunger is not a thing, Food Works more like an insta-heal item, This is Overpowered
and to balance this, you can't Stack Food 3. You can't Sprint, The fastest way to travel is minecarts
4. Bows are closer to machine guns
5. You can't put 2 chests next to eachother, you need atleast one block between then
6. If you are playing on an M-based Mac Computer, Version before 1.6 (including b1.7.3)
Your colors will be really mesed up (this is refered to as bluecraft)
a solution that works for some is forcing your game to fullscreen with cmd+shift+f11
(if you dont have fnlock on (weirdo) you need to press the fn key too)
